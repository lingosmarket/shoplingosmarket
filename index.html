Who We Are

Lingo's Market is a locally owned food market with an authentic French bakery. We specialize in offering a wide variety of groceries, meat, seafood, deli, produce, bakery, dairy, frozen food, ice cream, health and beauty items. Our store is a one-stop shop without the hassle of the big box stores. We have proudly served our community for over 100 years.

Where We Are

We are located in the Village By The Sea shopping complex conviently located directly off Rehoboth Ave between 1st and 2nd street.
